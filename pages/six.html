This shoot I did was a fun creative collaboration with Stephen J Mensah, an individual that I realized I share a lot in common with, like our initials (SJ). I found that to be something very interesting. That wholesome interaction made me think of how much of a phenomenon it is to meet random people who end up taking a huge part of your heart, and you taking a huge part of theirs. Compatibility in any kind of relationship is a huge feat in a world of 7 billion plus people, like the majesty and beauty of good friendship, meaningful relationships, and being seen and loved fully, as well as being given the opportunity to do the same! Makes one see how connection really is a gift from God.
