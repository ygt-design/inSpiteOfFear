For the past five years, I've been haunted by a recurring dream that finally ceased this year. That dream inspired me to create 'In Spite of Fear' as a reminder, both to myself and to others, to push forward despite the grip of fear. Fear has been a formidable force in my life, shaking my world in profound ways. However, during my trip to Ghana last year, I stood before the mirror in my childhood bedroom and made a promise to myself: I would not succumb to fear. I explicitly rejected anxiety, vowing that it would no longer hold sway over me.

In Spite of Fear is more than just a piece of art; it embodies a mindset, a way of life, a mantra to cling to when fear threatens to overwhelm. It serves as a beacon of courage and resilience, guiding us toward the life we yearn for. On the other side of fear lies the freedom we seek
