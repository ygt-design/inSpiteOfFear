Love, Fear and Everything
In—Between


An Exhibition by Steve Williams Jnr

Curated by Kris Lodu

Hosted at The Drake Hotel

Site by Yigit Toprak

Love, Fear and Everything In Between was born at a period when I was honestly filled with a lot of fear concerning… everything. I feel like I’m in the midst of so much change and this change has been happening over and over again. I never really had someone to hold my hand and guide me along this path and I’ve made so many mistakes. “In spite of fear”, a piece within the collection, was made as a constant reminder to myself to chase my dreams and to always belong to love, even amid fear.

Making and planning this work got me thinking about a lot. A lot is happening on the planet right now, both good and bad. I feel like it’s imperative that we make the best out of everything, take nothing at all for granted and remain intentional in everything. From our relationships to our utterances to our actions to the lives we lead.

Love, Fear and Everything In Between is in place as a reminder to always make love with equilibrium, embrace the chance of the pivot, relinquish control, belong [even more] to love, dwell where the flow resides, run back to you are at your core, and do it all in spite of fear.
